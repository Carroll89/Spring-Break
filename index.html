Sample Itinerary

This is just a draft so everyone has a simple starting point.

Tuesday • April 1

Arrival + Kalahari Day

  • Travel in and check into Kalahari
  • Spend the main part of the day at the waterpark
  • Keep the evening easy with food on-site and arcade / resort time

Wednesday • April 2

Train Ride or Easy Family Adventure

  • Top option: Lehigh Gorge Scenic Railway
  • Backup option: Costa’s Family Fun Park or shopping stop
  • Come back to Kalahari for swimming, relaxing, or dinner

Thursday • April 3

Nature / Activity Day + Steakhouse Night

  • Choose from Bushkill Falls, horseback riding, or a state park outing
  • Leave some room for downtime back at the resort
  • Dinner at the Kalahari steakhouse

Friday • April 4

Wrap-Up + Head Home

  • Easy breakfast and one last quick resort activity
  • Optional quick candy / souvenir stop if time works
  • Pack up and head home

Trip vibe

This trip should feel fun, easy, and memorable — not overplanned. Kalahari does the heavy lifting, and the area attractions are there to give us a few great options outside the resort.
